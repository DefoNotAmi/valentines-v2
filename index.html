How to add your images (two easy ways)

  • Way 1 (simple): Create a folder named images next to your index.html. Put your photos inside, for example: images/memory1.jpg. Then find the line like: <img src="" alt="..."> and change it to: <img src="images/memory1.jpg" alt="...">
  • Way 2 (click to upload): You can click on a photo box and pick an image. It will show instantly (good for preview). If you upload this page somewhere later, you’ll still want Way 1 for permanent images.
